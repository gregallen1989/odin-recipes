Smash Burgers

Description

If you like burgers then you will love this take on the classic formula. Thinner patties and extra cheese make for a delightful dinner.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef, 1lb
  • Hamburger Buns
  • American Cheese, slices
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Form thin patties with the hamburger meat. Cook in skillet.
  2. While patties are cooking, smash down with two spatchulas as thin as you can get them.
  3. Right before you pull the patties off the skillet, add cheese slices and stack them into double patties.
  4. Put patties on bun. Add lettuce.
  5. Enjoy!
