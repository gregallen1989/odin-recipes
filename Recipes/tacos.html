Street Tacos

Description

A modern take on a classic recipe, street tacos are perfect for your taco tuesdays.

Ingredients

  • Carne Asada, 1lb
  • Tortilla Shells
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Cook the Carne Assada in a skillet. Cut into strips.
  2. Warm tortillas in a skillet.
  3. Combine Carne Asada and lettuce into a tortilla. Add any other ingredients you might like as well.
  4. Enjoy
